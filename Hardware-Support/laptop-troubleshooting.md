# Laptop Troubleshooting

## Laptop Will Not Power On

Checklist

- Verify charger
- Check charging LED
- Hold power button for 15 seconds
- Remove external devices
- Test another charger

---

## Overheating

Possible Causes

- Dust buildup
- Blocked vents
- Failed fan
- High CPU usage

Solutions

- Clean vents
- Improve airflow
- Replace thermal paste if necessary
- Monitor temperatures

---

## Battery Problems

- Check battery health
- Update BIOS
- Calibrate battery
- Replace if degraded

---

## Display Issues

- Test external monitor
- Adjust brightness
- Update graphics driver
- Reseat display cable (service environment)
